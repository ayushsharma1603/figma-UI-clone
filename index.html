Why Choose
West Bay Surgery Center?

West Bay Surgery Center offers the benefits of an Ambulatory Surgery Center (ASC) — providing advanced outpatient procedures with the convenience, safety, and affordability that today’s patients deserve.

Our Difference
  • Same-day discharge after your procedure
  • Comfortable, patient-friendly environment
  • Convenient Tampa Bay location
  • Reduced infection risk compared to hospitals
  • Save up to 50% versus hospital-based surgeries

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Recovery time varies dedivending on your procedure and individual needs. Our team will provide guidance before discharge.

Family members may join patients, if they choose, up until the time of their procedure. At that time, we ask that family members remain in the waiting room until the procedure is complete. When you return to the recovery room and awaken, your family may once again join you. Parents will be asked to remain at the center for the entire time of any pediatric procedures so that they may be present as the child awakens. Your child’s comfort, by you the parent is an important part of recovery.

Our surgical team prepares equipment, monitors, and ensures safety throughout your procedure.

Please contact our support team for any questions before or after your surgery.

Arriving early allows for check-in, pre-op preparations, and ensures everything is ready for your procedure.
